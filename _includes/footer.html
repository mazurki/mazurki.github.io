I'm Marta and I apply psychology to UX. Need a coaching session? Let's talk!

I have a master's degree in Psychology, I finished Human Resource Management postgraduate studies and I'm a certified coach. I learned design principles and UX in practice working as a UX designer and researcher. It became my passion.

Here, I write about my experience and my perspective on the above issues - UX, design, and coaching. I hope you'll enjoy it. If you need support in achieving your goals, here you can also schedule the coaching session.

In my spare time, I do yoga, read books and sometimes draw or create analog collages. I love being in nature but ask me to visit a museum or gallery and I'm yours! Traveling is my second name.
